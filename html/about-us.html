Cafe Finder Copenhagen

Welcome to Cafe Finder Copenhagen

Discover and explore cafes in Copenhagen. Browse our list of cafes to find your favorite spots for coffee, pastries, and more.

We're a group of students, who were tired of never knowing where to go for studying.
So we took matters into our own hands, and built this website!

We took a minimalistic approach, using few tools to actually make the website. Due to it being a hobby project, the website keeps it
to a simple design and function. We provided what's needed, but are always open for ideas and other types of inquiries.

We're always trying to improve the website, but since we're doing it in our freetime, please have patience with potential fixes and add-ons.

Copenhagen is rich of cafes and places to sit and relax. So explore the website, and provide feedback if you have any.
